# Release Notes (v1.0)

Welcome to the grand launch of **Clash of Crowns**! Version 1.0 brings the ultimate single-player chess experience to your mobile device.

## What's New in v1.0:
- **Competitive Career Mode**: Battle your way through 8 offline tiers (Core, Beginner, Learner, Intermediate, Hard, Master, Grandmaster). 
- **Stockfish AI**: Challenge yourself against our deeply integrated Stockfish AI engine.
- **2D & 3D Environments**: Seamlessly switch between a classic 2D board or an immersive 3D apartment environment. We've heavily optimized the game to run smoothly on low-end devices.
- **Customization**: Earn coins by winning matches and use them to unlock brand-new board themes and piece sets!
- **Avatar Commentary**: Experience dynamic, real-time commentary from AI opponents as the match progresses.
- **Cloud Save & Leaderboards**: Never lose your progress! Sync your save to the cloud and compare your Comp Elo rating against players worldwide on the Global Leaderboard.

## Security & Stability
- Built-in forced update foundations to ensure all players stay on secure versions.
- Hardened offline save encryption and idempotency mechanisms to prevent accidental reward duplication.

## Coming Soon
- **Live Multiplayer**: Play against your friends in real-time.
- **Ranked Arena & Tournaments**: True competitive online matchmaking. 

Thanks for playing, and we'll see you on the board!
