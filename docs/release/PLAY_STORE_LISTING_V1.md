## Full Description
Welcome to **Clash of Crowns**, the definitive mobile chess experience blending classic strategy with deep progression. 

In version 1.0, hone your skills in our extensive offline **Competitive Career Mode**:
- **8 Tiers of Progression:** Start from Core and climb your way through Beginner, Learner, Intermediate, Hard, Master, and ultimately face the Crownless King in the Grandmaster Boss tier.
- **Dynamic AI Opponents:** Play against AI powered by the world-class Stockfish engine, directly embedded into the app. AI difficulty natively scales with your progression.
- **Customization & Rewards:** Earn coins and XP to unlock a variety of 2D and 3D board themes and piece sets. Receive milestone bonuses for clearing Master Cups!
- **Immersive 2D & 3D Environments:** Play seamlessly in 2D for quick moves or toggle to full 3D environments with dynamic lighting.
- **Offline Save & Cloud Sync:** Never lose your progress. Play offline on the subway, and safely sync your save to the cloud when you reconnect.

*Note: Multiplayer, Ranked Arena, and Tournaments are marked as "Coming Soon" and will be unlocked in upcoming free updates.*
