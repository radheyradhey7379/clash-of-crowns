## What to Test
We are focusing heavily on the single-player and core app stability for this launch. Please thoroughly test:
- **Offline Mode:** Turn off Wi-Fi/Cellular data and verify you can boot the game and play against AI without crashing.
- **Comp Career Mode:** Play through several AI opponents. Confirm rewards are granted and new tiers unlock properly.
- **2D & 3D Environments:** Switch between 2D and 3D modes during a match. Make sure the 3D board loads properly and does not lag your device excessively.
- **Save/Load:** Try closing the app entirely during a match or in the menu, and confirm your progress was saved.

## How Long to Test
We recommend a minimum of 15-20 minutes of continuous gameplay.
- **Performance Report:** Please report if your phone experiences extreme lag, app crashes, or excessive heating during the 3D matches.

## What is "Coming Soon"?
*Please do NOT report these as bugs.* The following features are intentionally locked down for this v1.0 launch:
- Multiplayer matches
- Ranked Arena
- Tournaments
- Challenge-to-Match features

## How to Report Bugs
Please use the `TESTER_FEEDBACK_TEMPLATE.md` format (or the provided QA form) when submitting bugs. Always include:
- Your exact device model (e.g., Pixel 7, Galaxy S21)
- Your Android version (e.g., Android 14)
- Network conditions (Online/Offline)
- Screenshots or screen recordings of the bug.

Thank you for helping us ensure Clash of Crowns is stable and flawless for the public launch!
